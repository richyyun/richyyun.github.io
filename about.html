Hi, I'm Richy Yun, Lead Neuroscientist at Sonera.

At Sonera I have been working on commercial biomagnetic applications of Sonera's aucoustic magnetic sensor.
Before Sonera I did my PhD in the Fetz lab at the University of Washington where I used intracortical electrode arrays to deliver stimulation and induce plasticity in the primate cortex. I have a wide array of experience including working with and performing surgery on various animal models, experimental design and setup, a multitude of analaysis techniques, and neural networks / machine learning.
I believe neurotech is at the cusp of emerging into a widely used platform, and I'm excited to be part of making it a reality!
